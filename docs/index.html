BPHT5 is a collection of HTML5 Templates to generate Business | Marketing Plans or project documentation. These are set styles and UI actions & animations for each template. There is NO server side or business logic here, just stand alone html which can be deployed or viewed with any standard tools. This collection has been assembeled to be HTML, CSS, JavaScrip nessasary to design and build business plan data to be presentable.

Executive Summary

Business Plans, Project Documentation, Marketing, are all reoccuring efforts and needed in the marketplace. JCSC.biz set out to build a few Business Plans and saw there is sad HTML5 planning templates or such things advailable. Once JCSC.biz had internal templates, it was decided to try and release these efforts as a tool set to help the community.

Mission Statment

Open Source. These templates are combanations of smaller working UI parts from our toolbox to build Single Page Designed Actionable Information, aka web application. There is no backend, that is for you to supply, V2 we may supply backend, but right now this just simple HTML documents and the supporting required charts, styles, scripts, images needed for these templates to function as designed.

Market Research

We looked when starting jcsc.biz for quality templates to help scaffold out our ideas into products. There were some options, no we will not waste time listing them as it can be a subjective problem. We were looking for HTML specific solutions, and specifically things that did not require a server or costly hosting. We utilze DNN and build custom sites from time to time, but those are not easily deployed for smaller ideas or may be cost prohibative from being able to manage the idea into a product. BPHT5 was built to help scaffold out ideas, plans, implimentation efforts, and other managment processes needed for project success. These are templates which we are utilizing in a server side platform, but also as simple starting point for documentation efforts.

Google Sites , NeoCities, GitHub & other places enable vitually free hosting environments. BPHT5 has scripts I can not yet get to work from google sites, and we will eventually have these templates there, but our main focus is making these templates more usable, & rewriting everything in Angular now that we have the usable html.

BPHT5 was thought of something to download to speed up documentation efforts. From Slide Shows, presentations, Business Plans, Charts & Reports there are many needs for start up organizations, projects and ideas to curate sucess. We are working to build a small platform to help showcase your data with out focusing on the technology, merly your data, yor project and building the content to support your idea.
